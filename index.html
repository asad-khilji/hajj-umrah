About Hajj & Umrah

Hajj is one of the Five Pillars of Islam and is mandatory for every able Muslim to perform at least once in their lifetime. Umrah is a voluntary pilgrimage, performed throughout the year.

Steps of Hajj & Umrah

  1. Ihram - Enter the state of spiritual purity.
  2. Tawaf - Circumambulate the Kaaba seven times.
  3. Sai - Walk between the hills of Safa and Marwah.
  4. Arafat - Stand at the plain of Arafat for prayer and reflection.
  5. Muzdalifah - Collect pebbles for the stoning of the pillars.
  6. Rami - Stone the pillars representing Satan.
  7. Tawaf al-Ifadah - Another circumambulation of the Kaaba.
